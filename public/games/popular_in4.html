静夜思

李白

床前明月光，疑是地上霜。
举头望明月，低头思故乡。
📖 英文翻译：

"Moonlight in front of my bed — I mistook it for frost on the ground. I raise my head to view the bright moon, then lower it, longing for my hometown."
